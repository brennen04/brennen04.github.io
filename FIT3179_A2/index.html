The Next Chapter: Jobs, Pay, and Reality After University

In Australia, the transition from university to the workforce is a significant milestone that shapes the future of graduates.
This visualization explores the relationship between university degrees, employment rates, and average salaries across various
fields of study.

Domestic students, who are Australian citizens or permanent residents, always have higher median salaries compared to
international students regardless the field of study and education level.
